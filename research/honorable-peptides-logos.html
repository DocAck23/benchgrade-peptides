How to give me feedback

Tell me which numbers you want to keep, kill, or refine. Refine requests can be anything:

  1. Style swap on the same concept — e.g., A1 but in the A5 forest-green/parchment colorway
  2. Colorway variant — e.g., A3 in oxblood + cream instead of charcoal
  3. Composition edit — e.g., B3 but replace the peptide glyph with a small sword
  4. Combine two — e.g., A4's geometric warrior on B6's typography layout
  5. New concept entirely — describe the feel and I'll generate more

Once you pick the direction, next step is: apply the winning logo to the actual vial labels, box packaging, and the website header so you can see how it sits in context. Right now these are just logos-in-a-square — the branded-system expression comes next.
