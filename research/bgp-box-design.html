F6 mark · gold foil · cartier maroon paperboard

Official Box Design

Three lid sizes (small, medium, large) — all maroon paperboard with the F6 mark foiled in antique gold. Cartier discipline: no other text on the lid. Production-ready SVG artwork at native dimensions, ready to send to the labeler.

Spec

Box construction

Hardcover Gift Box

Construction
Rigid paperboard hardcover, lift-off lid (telescope style — lid sits on top of base, no hinge)
Wall thickness
2mm rigid board, paper-wrapped on both interior and exterior
Exterior color
#5C1A1A (cartier maroon) · matte uncoated wrap, no laminate
Interior color
#5C1A1A (matching maroon) on lid interior · cream foam tray inside the base
Tray
Cream EVA foam (#F2EAD9), die-cut cavities sized per pack count
Lid art
F6 mark foil-stamped in antique gold (#B8923A). Single-pass hot foil, raised slightly above the paperboard surface.
No printed text
The lid carries the mark only. No wordmark, no website, no copy. Discipline.
