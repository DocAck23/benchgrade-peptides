The Founder's Letter · Embossed Seal Aesthetic

Cartier COA card meets old-world correspondence. Customers should look forward to opening this every month.
Suite · sleeve + card
Letter open · desk context
Embossed seal · macro detail

Monthly cadence

Each month: a new short calligraphy letter from the founder. Same sleeve, same embossed seal, different note. The card itself is the "newsletter" — tactile, signed, never sent by email. Customers who order frequently start collecting them.

Optional escalation: number each month's card as a numbered edition (e.g. "April 2026 · No. 0042 / 5000") to make the collectible aspect explicit.
